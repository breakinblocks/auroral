# Auroral 1.0.1

## Auroral Nautilus

The Auroral Nautilus can now be tamed and ridden:

- Tame with Aurora Shards (33% chance per shard)
- Tamed nautilus can be saddled for flight
- Flying controls: WASD for movement, look up/down to ascend/descend
- Nautilus now only drops Aurora Shards when killed by a player

## Guide

- Renamed guide to "The Auroral Guidebook" with custom texture

## Textures

- Updated artwork for several item and entity textures
- Updated Shimmersteel Ingot texture
- Updated Auroral Nautilus texture

## Advancements

Added a complete advancement tree to guide players through the mod's progression:

### Root & Core Progression

- **Auroral** - Root advancement (enter the overworld)
- **Celestial Gift** - Collect an Aurora Shard
- **Frozen Forge** - Craft a Glacial Basin
- **Celestial Metal** - Create a Shimmersteel Ingot through aurora infusion
- **Woven Light** - Create Shimmerweave Fabric through aurora infusion

### Equipment Milestones

- **Ice Armory** - Craft all Shimmersteel tools (pickaxe, axe, shovel, hoe, sword, bow)
- **Aurora Clad** - Craft a full set of Shimmerweave armor
- **Northern Star** - Craft a Shimmer Spear

### Exploration & Utility

- **Cold Alchemy** - Craft a Cold Brewing Stand
- **Night Vision** - Harvest a Glow-Leek
- **Hearth and Home** - Obtain a Hearthwood Log
- **Guiding Light** - Craft an Aurora Lantern

### Creatures

- **Frozen Companion** - Tame an Auroral Snowlette
- **Sky Sailor** - Ride an Auroral Nautilus

### Challenges

- **Light in the Darkness** - Slay a Warden while holding an Aurora Lantern (hidden challenge)
